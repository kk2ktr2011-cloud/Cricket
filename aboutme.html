About Me

My name is Keshav Korattur. I am in highschool currently, and I enjoy playing Cricket, coding, and playing video games. I decided to build this project for fun and for this internship, as Cricket is my favorite sport and teaching it would be nice. I started watching Cricket in 2019 during the ICC ODI WC. After that, I played Cricket for a bit. During the summer of 2025, I started playing Cricket with my friends a lot more than I usually did. From then on, I have played Cricket at least once a week. Overall, Cricket is my favorite sport of all time so incorporating this with my coding skills makes me happy.

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to teach people about essential skills to play Cricket. While they aren't completely conclusive, they will serve as a base for anyone who wants to start out. Some of these skills are advanced as well for those who want to learn them.
